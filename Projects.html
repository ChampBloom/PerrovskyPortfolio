About Me

Achim is a small project I did for my college. The assignment was to create a nation within a world and make its inhabitants and their weapons and lifestyles. Achim is all about functional ecosystems. The top predator is a species of parasitic humanoid fungus that have reached sentiency. I wanted to give fairies a darker twist.
